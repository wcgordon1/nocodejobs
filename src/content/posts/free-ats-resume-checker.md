## What an ATS Actually Does

### Beginner Explanation

An Applicant Tracking System:
- Extracts text from your resume  
- Identifies job titles and dates  
- Matches keywords against job descriptions  
- Scores relevance  

If your formatting is broken, the system may misread sections entirely.

### Technical Depth

ATS software tokenizes text, normalizes terminology, and assigns weighted match scores based on job description alignment. Multi column formatting, text inside graphics, and decorative layouts often reduce parsing accuracy.

If you built your resume in Canva with design blocks and icons, there is a high chance it is not parsing cleanly.
