## How Applicant Tracking Systems Process Cover Letters

### Beginner Explanation

An ATS scans your letter to:

- Extract text  
- Identify job titles  
- Match keywords  
- Store searchable content  

If your letter does not reference the exact job title or relevant skills, it weakens your alignment.

### Technical Depth

Some systems index cover letters in searchable candidate databases. Recruiters later query skills and keywords. If your cover letter omits critical terminology found in the job description, you reduce retrieval probability.

Use the [PopResume Cover Letter Tool](https://www.popresume.com/free-cover-letter-checker) to validate keyword alignment before submission.

## Real World Example

### Weak Version

“I am very excited to apply for this opportunity and believe I would be a great fit.”

Why it fails:
- No job title  
- No measurable result  
- No company reference  

### Strong Version

“I am applying for the Backend Engineer position at Acme Corp. In my current role, I developed Node.js APIs serving 120k monthly users and reduced database latency by 37 percent.”

Why it works:
- References exact role  
- Includes metrics  
- Reinforces technical stack  
- Aligns directly to likely job keywords
